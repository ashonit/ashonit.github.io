"How to Build a Computer"
Course Overview

Ever wanted to learn how to build your own PC?
Or even just get a better idea of what all the parts
are inside and how they fit together, in order to perform
basic maintenance or upgrades?

Starting with the core parts in a modern computer
and how to choose them to meet your budget, we go
right through to putting it together, testing and
software installation.

Building your own computer is a satisfying experience —
not to mention fun.

The course can cater for up to 10 people. It is broken
into 6 sessions of approximately 1 – 1.5 hours each.
